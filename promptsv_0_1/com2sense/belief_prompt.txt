Q: Jane loved to upset her parents with loud noises so she threw a paper plate on the floor?
A: A paper plate is light and will not make any noise when thrown to the floor. Therefore, the statement is false.

A: Stars are seen more clearly when it's dark. Therefore, the statement is true.

A: Caffeine is a stimulant and will wake you up. Therefore, the statement is true.

Q: A man who can bench press two hundred pounds can easily lift a small child?
A: A small child typically weighs way less than 200 pounds. Therefore, the statement is true.
